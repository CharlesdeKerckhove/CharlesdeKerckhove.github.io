Who am I?

Let me tell you what makes me, well me.

I am a confident, self-motivated web designer who is keen to work within a team of other talented, creative and passionate colleagues. I have excellent written and verbal communication skills whilst also being a systematical problem solver who enjoys challenges and works well under pressure.

Moreover, I am a second year computing student that is hoping to gain further experience in the web design industry to further my education. Learning whilst on the job would expand my knowledge of the work required and processes that businesses within the industry use from day-to-day.

I love being outdoors and therefore I jumped at the opportunity to take part in the Duke of Edinburgh Silver and Bronze awards in 2014 & 2013 respectively. I am trained in Emergency First Aid by St John's Ambulance and gained a FA Level 1 Coaching Award so I could coach my local football team in my spare time. Alongside coaching football, I competed for over ten years both at university and for local teams where I held the role of captain for my last two years.
