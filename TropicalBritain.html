Tropical Britain

Whilst working a part-time job for this company, I provided guidance on the social media accounts regarding how to engage the target audience, increase followers and create an Instagram account. The Instagram account was particularly important as showing off the amazing quality of each plant via photos inspires all types of people, from horticulturist to casual gardeners. When people are inspired to create their own tropical garden it increases demand.

The owner and I also worked tightly to extend my knowledge of search engine optimization (SEO) to create organic growth. This was done through intelligent keywords, up-to-date content and relevant page content/titles. The Tropical Britain website is constantly a top result in searches regarding tropical plants.
