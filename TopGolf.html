Top Golf

I was tasked with the job of creating a training application for TopGolf Surrey, they desired a quiz-style mobile app that would have a database of questions relevant to each job role (bar staff, kitchen, runners etc). This project was done by a team of four where I held the role of both designer and developer. An agile approach was taken due to the close connections to the company allowing constant reviews and ideas to be shared continuously.

The final prototype was then presented by the group to a panel where we had to explain how it worked, why it was an improvement on the current system and reflect on the task and what issues we overcome. To briefly explain how we answered these questions the screenshots show how the databases are structured - they have a bank of questions, answers (correct and incorrect) and employee details.
