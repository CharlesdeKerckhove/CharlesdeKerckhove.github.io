Longacres

During my time on work experience at Longacres Garden Center I worked with the internet department. This involved updating the website and as shown above, lead to the creation of a small animal products webpage focusing on the new range being introduced to the online store. This was primarily done through their SAGE system. Images had to be found, product descriptions created and stock levels checked and published to the webpage.

Other roles included restructuring the VAT codes of every item on the website as the company was paying tax on tax-free products. Moreover, it became my role to monitor their social media accounts replying to messages, comments and oversee a Facebook competition focused around James Wellbeloved Dog Food.
